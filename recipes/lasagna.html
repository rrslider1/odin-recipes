Yummy Lasagna

The Most Italian Dish on the Planet

In this town we love clown pie, and why not love Clown pie, it truly has a funny smell :)

Ingredients

  • Clown Tomatoes
  • Loads of horseradish
  • A sprinkle of Cyanide
  • A parcel of Sugar Plums

The Italian Process

  1. First stick your finger in the Clown Tomatoes and mess them up a little bit
  2. Once the clown tomatoes are fully messed up, you will be able to put them in a big pot
  3. The pot must boil to at least 1000 degress celcius, or hotter than the dman sun, whichever is warmer to the touch
  4. Wait 65 hours, and you will have the best clown soup in the world
  5. Wait 650 hours and you will have the best clown lasagna know to man
